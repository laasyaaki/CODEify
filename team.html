FOUNDER/EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR, STRATEGY
Andrew Romitti

Andrew is a rising senior at Rockbridge High School in Columbia, MO. He has been programming for 9 years and is passionate in AI and software. He created a software that diagnoses dementia in MRI SCANs which won a local congressional app challenge and was featured in multiple news outlets. He also is currently publishing research regarding using AI to create synthetic MRI Scans for dementia diagnosis for the international conference for synthetic datasets.
