Frequently Asked Questions

    Is CODEify a nonprofit? Yes! CODEify is a 501(c)(3) (in progress lol) certified organization.
    Do tutors earn volunteer hours? Yes! Tutors earn 1 volunteer hour for every class they tutor.
    How much do classes cost? Absolutely nothing! As a nonprofit dedicated to teaching children how to program, we want to make our classes accessible to everyone.
    What do I need to take a CODEify class? All of our classes are held online, once a week. No prior experience is necessary to participate!
