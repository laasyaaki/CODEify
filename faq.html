Frequently Asked Questions
Who is CODEify for?
CODEify is for any young student interested in learning about coding and technology, regardless of background, or prior experience. More can be found in our mission.
Does a student need any prior coding experience to join these programs?
No prior coding experience is required. Our classes are created to teach beginners the fundamentals.
Are classes free?
Yes! As a nonprofit organization dedicated to making coding education accessible to all, we firmly believe that a crucial step towards realizing our mission involves providing this knowledge without any financial barriers.
Do you offer certificates for completing classes?
Yes, we offer personalized certificates after the end of each course to every student that successfully participated.
What's your approach to fostering a passion for coding?
We take a hands-on approach to introduce the world of coding by using real-word examples which students will be inspired to work towards, practical projects which help students use and grow their skills, and tutors who are passionate about this subject.
Do tutors receive volunteer hours for teaching?
Yes! We will verify all hours spent volunteering
Can I volunteer with CODEify?
Yes! If you are a high school student or older, you can apply to be a tutor using this link.
How can I support this organization?
You can support this organization by spreading the word and supporting us on social media. Also, you can support us with feedback so that we can improve CODEify and work towards our mission.
